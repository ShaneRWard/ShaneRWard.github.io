Squarespace Website Building Platform

Squarespace is an easy to use, all-in-one platform for building websites and will host your website for a monthly fee plus domain cost. It provides templates and themes to build a great looking, fully functional site. You can even add an online store for an added fee. Follow these steps to get started on your own website:

Getting Started

  1. 1. Sign up.
  2. 2. Answer questions regarding website: goal, site content, site description, title.
  3. 3. Services tab, under Main Navigation, has lots of demo content.
  4. 4. Content editable in real time.
  5. 5. May add online store through Squarespace, but may cost more.
  6. 6. When website is the way you like, click upgrade to choose a website or commerce plan to publish website.
  7. 7. Custom domain can be added from settings/domains. Can buy one through Squarespace, or link to one you already have.

Now, Squarespace is not the only web building platform out there and if you are more experienced with web development or you have a particular design in mind, it may not be for you. Listed here are the pros and cons of using Squarespace:

Pros

  • Hosted solution, they take care of everything.
  • Impressive set of designs, all organized by purpose
  • Has a great site builder that guides you in a visual way.
  • Has lots of built-in features such as blogs, ecommerce modules, etc.

    • Cons

  • You don't control the site. It is at Squarespace's discression to shut down your page or it could disappear at any time.
  • There are similar designs that make a lot of assumptions.
  • Customization is limited by what the platform offers.
