Payment Setup

There are no doubt lots of people who would love to run a business from a website, making money at any and all times of the day or night, and perhaps most attractive of all, to do it on their own time. The problem is, they likely have no idea how or even where to begin. Even those who are skilled enough to build a web page will look at the idea of an online store as something on the next level. It may, however, not be as daunting a task as it originally seems.

The following guide will direct you to available options and explain the strentchs and weaknesses (if they exist) of each. There are five options available some more complicated than others, but for the purpose of smaller online stores, there are solutions available that are simpler than those needed for a larger store.

5 Categories

  1. 1. True Merchant Account Provider Hosted Payment Page
  2. 2. All-In-One Processor (aka "PSP") Hosted Payment Page
  3. 3. Custome Pages Using API Integration (Hosted on your domain)
  4. 4. eCommerce or "Cart" Solutions (Online Stores).
  5. 5. CRM, Form Builders, and Payment Page Generators

1. True Merchant Account Provider Hosted Payment Page

Pros

  • Hosted solution, they take care of everything.
  • Impressive set of designs, all organized by purpose
  • Has a great site builder that guides you in a visual way.
  • Has lots of built-in features such as blogs, ecommerce modules, etc.

    • Cons

  • You don't control the site. It is at Squarespace's discression to shut down your page or it could disappear at any time.
  • There are similar designs that make a lot of assumptions.
  • Customization is limited by what the platform offers.
